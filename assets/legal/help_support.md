## How to Contact Support

We offer multiple ways to get help:

### Live Chat (Primary Channel)
The fastest way to get assistance is through our **in-app Live Chat**. You can access it directly from:
- The Settings screen → Help & Support → Live Chat
- The profile menu → Support
- Any screen with the help icon

Our chat support may be AI-assisted to provide you with quick answers, but complex issues are escalated to our human support team.

### Email Support
For non-urgent matters or detailed inquiries, you can reach us at:
**support@waylo.app**

We typically respond within 24-48 hours during business days.

## Troubleshooting

Before contacting support, try these quick fixes:

### App Not Loading
1. Check your internet connection
2. Force close and restart the app
3. Update to the latest version
4. Clear app cache (Settings → Storage)

### Sync Issues
1. Ensure you're logged in
2. Check internet connectivity
3. Try logging out and back in
4. Verify your account status

### AI Chat Not Responding
1. Check your subscription status
2. Verify internet connection
3. Try starting a new chat
4. Check if you've reached daily limits (free tier)

### Expense Tracking Problems
1. Ensure all required fields are filled
2. Check currency settings
3. Verify date format
4. Try refreshing the expenses list

### Login Problems
1. Verify your email and password
2. Check for typos
3. Use "Forgot Password" if needed
4. Ensure your account isn't locked

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account, or contact support@waylo.app for assistance.

### How do I export my data?
Navigate to Settings → Privacy → Export My Data. You'll receive a download link via email within 48 hours.

### Can I get a refund for my subscription?
Please see our Terms of Service or contact support to discuss refund eligibility based on your specific situation.

### Is my financial data secure?
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and never store sensitive payment information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

### Can I use the app offline?
Some features work offline, but AI chat, currency conversion, and sync require an internet connection.

### What languages does the app support?
Waylo supports multiple languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Hebrew, Arabic, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Italian, Portuguese, and Russian.
