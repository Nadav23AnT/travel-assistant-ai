## 6. Data Security

We take security seriously and implement industry-standard measures:

### 6.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption in transit**: All data transmitted using TLS/SSL encryption
- **Encryption at rest**: Sensitive data encrypted in our databases
- **Secure authentication**: Password hashing with industry-standard algorithms
- **Access controls**: Role-based permissions for internal team members

### 6.2 Organizational Safeguards
- Regular security audits and monitoring
- Incident response procedures
- Employee training on data protection
- Limited access to personal data (need-to-know basis)

### 6.3 Your Responsibility
- Keep your password secure and confidential
- Log out from shared devices
- Report suspicious activity immediately
- Enable two-factor authentication (when available)

**No system is 100% secure.** While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use strong passwords and exercise caution.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

**Active Accounts**
- Data retained while your account is active
- AI chat history may be retained for service improvement (anonymized where possible)

**Deleted Accounts**
- Most personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- Some data retained for legal compliance (e.g., transaction records for tax/audit purposes)
- Aggregated/anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for analytics

**Backups**
- Data in backups may persist for up to 90 days after deletion

You can request specific retention information by contacting support@waylo.app.
